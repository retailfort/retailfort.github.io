RetailFort POS can handle high transactions. Enabled with outstanding productivity and increased efficiency at point of sale billing counters.

GST Ready

RetailFort is committed in meeting client expectations with updates for GST well in time. Generate multiple returns to file with GSTN. Invoices and receipts are GST compliant. Make your business ready for GST.

Sale Management

Retail / Wholesale billing supported with option to manage customer credit sales. Customer management made easy. Sales operations are made faster than ever with options to Edit, Exchange, Reprint, & Cancel Bill along with parallel billing.

Inventory Management

Complete solution to manage your inventory at affordable cost. Maintain your stock ledger, supplier management, product tax management, batch management and much more… Stock update is never been easier. With bulk upload option with click of a button, upload all your product stock in a jiffy.

Weighing Scale Integration

RETAILFORT integrates with all weighing scales by different brands worldwide allowing you to add direct weight measurement into the POS screen and suits fruit and vegetable business. You can now simply put the items on a scale, press the desired stock item on screen and it will be weighed instantly & price will be displayed on screen

Barcode Scanning

For the fast and secure entry of stock items into the sales are made using barcode scanner. It's also used on customer accounts for scanning membership cards, scanning bar tabs, and for scanning employee's accounts to log onto a sale.

Label Printing

RETAILFORT helps to print out on mediums & save your time from multiple searches with barcode scanners. It helps you to select multiple items in one go and Stock Items, Customers and Promotions can be printed using various label settings. Add a scan code to all stock items automatically if required and also helps to scan easily during a stock take.

Proximity Marketing & Promotions

Use Proximity Marketing to share digital contents to mobile devices of customers who pass by your shop within a particular distance or proximity. Beacon app enables brands and businesses to notify offers and promotions to users who pass by within the range of your Fruit & Vegetable Stores.

Loyalty function

With RETAILFORT, you can Implement various loyalty scheme for your loyal customers and preset discount rate for repeat customers & reward customers by accumulating points to spend in their next bill. Points can be consolidated for chain stores hence you are hassle-free about the management of loyalty program
